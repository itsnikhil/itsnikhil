About Wisdom Abacus Society

Wisdom Educational Society (WES) was established in 2009 by Senior Educationist and others, with an objective of internationally accepted quality & technical concept based educational program in India and providing creative skill education to the society. It is an educational society registered under the Society's Registration No. 216/2009-10, Faridabad.

The Wisdom Educational. Society has done pioneering work in the field of education. Within a span of 5 years the Wisdom Society from a humble beginning of more than 20 centres in Faridabad, Gurgaon, Palwal and Delhi.

The Society has made tremendous progress in the field of academics, mental & creative activities. The students take maximum advantage of these courses and have shown great results by participating in the State and National level competitions . The mental and creative education enhances the personality of our students and develops self-confidence, self respect and a positive attitude towards life.
